About Me

Front End Developer

I recently decided that it's time to change my career, and become a Web Developer. I always had a curiosity and fascination about various aspects of technology like responsive websites and realistic games.

I quickly became to understand that code is a superpower and every person should be able to access it. Understanding that code is the underlying(and invisible)framework of tech means that we do not have to be passive bystanders in our ever changing digital world.

To gain skills and knowledge in this industry, I enrolled with Just IT, being trained in becoming a professional Web Developer.
